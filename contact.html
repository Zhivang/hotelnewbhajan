Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept online payments for room bookings?

Yes, we accept online payments through various methods including credit cards, debit cards, and UPI. You can also pay at the time of check-in. For advance bookings, we require 30% advance payment.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we provide complimentary parking for all our guests. We have a spacious parking area that can accommodate cars, SUVs, and two-wheelers. Valet parking service is also available for events and banquet guests.

Can I book your restaurant for a private party?

Absolutely! We offer private dining arrangements in our restaurant for smaller groups (up to 50 people). For larger events, our banquet hall is the perfect choice. Contact our events team to discuss your requirements and get a customized quote.

Do you offer home delivery for your restaurant menu?

Currently, we do not offer direct home delivery service. However, you can order through popular food delivery platforms like Zomato and Swiggy, or call us for takeaway orders which you can pick up from our restaurant.

What is your cancellation policy for banquet bookings?

For banquet bookings, we require at least 15 days advance notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations made between 7-14 days will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 7 days before the event are non-refundable, though we offer rescheduling options subject to availability.
