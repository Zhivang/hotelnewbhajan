Established 1995

A Journey of Flavor and Hospitality

Hotel New Bhajan was founded in 1995 by Mr. Rajesh Kumar with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a place where travelers could find not just a meal or a bed, but a true home away from home. What began as a modest establishment with just a handful of rooms and a small dining area has blossomed into one of Unnao's most beloved landmarks.

Our strategic location under the Hardoi Overbridge on Station Road was chosen deliberately. We wanted to be easily accessible to both local residents and travelers passing through this vibrant city. Over the years, this location has become synonymous with quality dining and comfortable accommodations in the region.

While we take pride in our comfortable rooms and modern amenities, it is our restaurant that has truly captured the hearts of Unnao. Our kitchen, led by experienced chefs who have been with us for decades, continues to serve the authentic North Indian flavors that made us famous. The secret to our success is simple: fresh ingredients, traditional recipes passed down through generations, and an unwavering commitment to hygiene and quality.
