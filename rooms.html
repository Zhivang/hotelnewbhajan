Important Information

Booking Policies

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is from 12:00 PM onwards. Early check-in is subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late check-out can be arranged with prior notice and is subject to room availability.

Valid government-issued photo identification is required at the time of check-in for all guests.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before the scheduled check-in date. Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in will be charged for one night's stay.

No-shows will be charged for the entire booking duration. We recommend booking flexible rates if your travel plans are uncertain.

Payment Options

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments. Full payment is required at the time of check-in unless advance payment has been made during booking.

Online advance booking is available with a 30% advance payment option. Contact us for corporate billing arrangements.

Children Policy

Children below 5 years stay free of charge when sharing existing bedding with parents. Children between 5-12 years are charged 50% of the room rate.

Extra beds or cribs can be provided on request at an additional charge. Please inform us in advance for better arrangements.
