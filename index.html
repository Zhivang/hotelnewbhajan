More Than Just A Hotel

Located strategically under the Hardoi Overbridge on Station Road, New Bhajan has become a landmark destination in Unnao. While our comfortable rooms offer a peaceful sanctuary for travelers, it is our restaurant that has truly captured the hearts and taste buds of the entire city.

We take immense pride in serving authentic North Indian cuisine prepared with the finest ingredients in our meticulously maintained hygienic kitchen. Our warm, family-friendly atmosphere combined with decades of culinary excellence has made dining with us an essential part of any visit to Unnao.
