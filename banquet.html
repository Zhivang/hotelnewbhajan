Premium Event Venue

Celebrate in Style

Our spacious AC banquet hall is the perfect setting for weddings, engagement ceremonies, birthday celebrations, corporate meetings, and social gatherings. With a capacity to accommodate up to 200 guests, we provide a versatile space that can be customized to suit your event requirements.

Located in the heart of Unnao under the Hardoi Overbridge, our venue offers easy accessibility for your guests with ample parking facilities. Our experienced event management team works closely with you to ensure every detail is perfect, from decoration to dining, making your special day truly memorable.

We take pride in our full-service catering that features our famous North Indian cuisine, customizable to your preferences and dietary requirements. Our culinary team can create special menus that will delight your guests and make your event a gastronomic success.
